What's Beknowin'?

beknow however is a very old word meaning

"to become acquainted with; to be aware of, conscious of..."

...So that's quite nice... happy to beknowin' you and make your acquaintance..

[hmm sounds a bit Worzel Gummidge to me!]


Anyway....why?
